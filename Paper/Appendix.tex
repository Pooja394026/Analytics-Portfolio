\section{Appendix}

\textbf{Data Sources}

The data sources used in this analysis can be found at the following locations: (1) Motor Vehicle Collisions – Crashes: \url{https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Public-Safety/Motor-Vehicle-Collisions-Crashes/h9gi-nx95/about_data}, (2) Motor Vehicle Collisions – People: \url{https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Public-Safety/Motor-Vehicle-Collisions-Person/f55k-p6yu/about_data}, (3) Motor Vehicle Collisions – Vehicles: \url{https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Public-Safety/Motor-Vehicle-Collisions-Vehicles/bm4k-52h4/about_data}, and (4) NOAA Weather Data via API: \url{https://data.meteostat.net}.
